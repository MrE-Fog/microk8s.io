Whilst Kubernetes as a platform has numerous benefits, the learning curve for enterprises is steep. This is a barrier to adoption, impacting ISVs that look at containers as an efficient delivery mechanism for their software.

As a software vendor, embedding MicroK8s is the easiest way to leverage the benefits of a modern, Kubernetes-based architecture with simple customer adoption, removing infrastructure dependencies and ensuring software will run anywhere predictably.

Better customer experiences

MicroK8s is lightweight, conformant Kubernetes, with sensible defaults that 'just work'. A quick install, easy upgrades and great security make it perfect for micro clouds and edge computing.

Easier deployments

  • Faster, appliance-style deployment of infra and apps
  • Reference platform setup simplifies ongoing maintenance
  • Cloud, Kubernetes, edge, infrastructure

Dedicated platforms improve operations

MicroK8s is the easiest way to consume Kubernetes as it abstracts away much of the complexity of managing the lifecycle of clusters. Its low-touch UX automates or simplifies operations such as deployment, clustering, and enabling of auxiliary services required for a production-grade K8s environment.

  • Full control over resources and workloads
  • No noisy neighbours

Exceptional

MicroK8s is a zero-ops Kubernetes for delivering your software. Suitable for clouds, edge and IoT clusters, #MicroK8s offers a self-healing high availability feature for enterprise-grade deployments.

  • Fire-and-forget K8s with transactional over-the-air updates
  • Self-healing high availability
  • GPU acceleration and other add-ons

As you want it

With MicroK8s you get a complete, CNCF-compliant Kubernetes environment and all the services you need to run your OCI containers at scale, such as networking & load balancing, storage, service mesh, observability, GPU & FPGA acceleration, multi-cluster management and more – unique among lightweight Kubernetes distributions.

  • CNCF-conformant
  • Platform independent & OCI compliant
  • Hardened for regulatory compliances
